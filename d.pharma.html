COURSES

D.PHARM

PROGRAMME OBJECTIVE :

A person who has completed D. Pharm can be employed as a Registered Pharmacist in a community pharmacy (Medical Store/Chemists & Druggists) or a hospital
pharmacy. It is the minimum qualification to practice pharmacy; therefore, it is mandatory that all dispensing of prescription medicines has to be done
under the supervision of a pharmacist only. He/She can also work as a medical representative & also work in an industry.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

The Candidate should be Passed in 10+2 examination in Science with Physics, and Chemistry as compulsory subjects
along with one of the subjects: Mathematics or Biology (Botany and Zoology) from a recognised institution in U.P
State or outside U.P. State.

PROGRAMME DURATION :

Duration of the course is 2 years with each academic year spread over not less than 180 working days. A candidate
needs to complete a training of minimum 500 hrs. Spread over a period of 3 months after completion of second year
of the course..

ADMISSION PROCEDURE :

For admission to D. Pharm the eligible candidate will register himself/herself through either online mode at website
portal or manually after coming to the college. After investigating the application, candidate seeking admission
will be called for the interview. He/she will produce necessary documents including Aadhar card, provisional or
original certificates of qualification required for the admission. With approval of the competent authority,
candidate will pay the admission fees as per the college norms.
