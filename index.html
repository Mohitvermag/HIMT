HIMT MISSION AND VISION AND QUALITY POLICY

HOME - ABOUT - MISSION VISION QUALITY POLICY



HIMT College of Pharmacy was established in the year 2005. The College is running D Pharma, B. Pharma and M. Pharma courses
with the approval of AICTE as also Pharmacy Council of India (PCI). HIMT College of pharmacy is affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul
Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. The College also ensures continuous placement of the eligible students in top Pharma
companies. Well-equipped laboratories of Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacognosy, Machine Room,
Anatomy and Physiology, Central Instrumentation Facility (CIF) and an animal house have been established for enabling the
students to gain experimental knowledge. The college has been utilizing all its resources to equip students with knowledge
through the latest teaching tools such as lectures, study projects, tutorials, seminars etc. and by providing students
suitable platforms for interaction with eminent industrial professionals.
The campus has been beautifully landscaped with extensive greenery & plantation in a pollution free environment.
We have successfully completed one and half decade long journey and feel proud to achieve reputation and good
will in the field of Pharmacy. The array of best of the required facilities created in the campus offers limitless
opportunities to the students to explore their innate capabilities, and to develop the passion for lifelong learning.

VISION

HIMT college of Pharmacy was established in year 2005 with purpose to produce best Pharmacist & good citizen of country. The visionary
management have focused on specific goal & to contribute toward society. To realise the dream practically on ground the Pharmacy
College was established in year 2005 with B. Pharmacy course with all approval.
The Institute is well furnished & equipped with latest instruments as per standard guideline. The college started Diploma in
Pharmacy in year 2015 to produced registered pharmacist for healthcare system. The continues growth & success stories inspired
management to start with Masters Degree course. The dream was accomplished in year 2011 with M.Pharmacy Pharmaceutics & in year
2013 with M.Pharmacy Pharmacology.
To be a centre of excellence in Pharmaceutics research & to produce the entrepreneur who read the pharmaceutical business.
