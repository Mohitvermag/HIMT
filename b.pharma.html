COURSES

B.PHARM

Bachelor of Pharmacy is a 4-year programme, divided equally into 8 semesters, offered at the graduate level in India. It is the essential and fundamental requirement for
stepping in the field of Pharmacy. As per the present curriculums in India, B. Pharm degree mainly prepares the candidates to work in the pharmaceutical industry or to
practice pharmacy in a community or hospital pharmacy.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

For Admission to first year of B. Pharm.- Candidate must have passed intermediate/10+2 level
examination or its equivalent from any recognized board/University; securing minimum 45% (40% SC/ST)
with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with any one of Mathematics/Bio-Technology/Computer Science/Biology.

For Admission to second year of B. Pharm. (Lateral entry) Candidate should have passed 2years Diploma in Pharmacy
from recognised college/institute with minimum 60% marks.

PROGRAMME DURATION:

Duration of the course is 4 years equally divided into 8 semesters. A candidate needs to complete
150 hours spread over four weeks in a Pharmaceutical Industry/Hospital..

ADMISSION PROCEDURE:

For admission to B. Pharm the eligible candidate will register himself/herself through either
online mode at website portal or manually after coming to the college. After investigating the
application, candidate seeking admission will be called for the interview. He/she will produce
necessary documents including Aadhar card, provisional or original certificates of qualification
required for the admission. With approval of the competent authority, candidate will pay the admission
fees as per the college norms..
